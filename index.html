How To Play!

QUICK BEFORE THE MARIOS ESCAPE!

  • On desktop, use your mouse to hit the marios before they escape from the pipes!
  • On mobile, tap the marios to hit them!
  • You have 30 seconds to hit as many marios as you can and save the world
  • Each time you get a successful hit of the mario, you will get one point!
